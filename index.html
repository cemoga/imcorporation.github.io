    WHO WE ARE

    An English executive teaching centre, with a total applied and last generation pragmatic emphasis, to contribute in a decisive manner to the achievement of our students’ personal and professional goals. We possess the necessary academic and corporate experience to guide you with complete effectiveness. We are a company with the prospect of diversification in the short mid-term.

    WHY TO CHOOSE US

    We comprehend the needs of our students and maximise the use of the English language teaching resources available;



    Adaptability

    Our Methodology is designed to learn the language under the conditions of our students, in the personal, academic and professional fields, and therefore we know what works and the most effective way to transmit it, taking the best from all the approaches seen in a long trajectory and complementing them appropriately.

    Pedagogy

    Our methodology has not been chosen with a commercial interest but from the student’s maximum pedagogical benefit point of view.

    Respect

    Our policy is centred in the respect and appreciation of all our internal and external clients in our commercial relations but also from the human point of view, in order to procure a warm organisational climate so that everyone can feel they are Living The Dream of their lives.

    Honesty

    Honesty and to keep one’s word are our main values.

    METHODOLOGY

    The orthodox methodologies have solid technical basis but lack of the applicability required by a language. On the other hand, the conversational methods tend to get away from a structured plan and overlook complementary or innovative elements discovered and implemented by the latest language pedagogical tendencies. After a careful analysis an search, we found the ideal balance between these two approaches.
